About Us
We are the GCB (Grad Center Bar), a members-only not-for-profit serving the greater Brown University community.
Visiting Us
To visit you must:
Be 21 years of age or older.
Have a current (unexpired) Brown ID OR a current COVID Vaccine Card with at least 2 doses received.
Have a valid (unexpired & legible) ID for proof of age as required by the State of Rhode Island. The actual document or card must be present, no copies will be accepted.
Membership
To be a member you must be part of the greater Brown University community or sponsored by a current member.
